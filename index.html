About Me

I’m interested in how we can design vehicles that understand their environment well enough to make reliable decisions on their own. A lot of my work revolves around simulating flight dynamics, designing controllers, and writing the software that sits between theory and hardware.

I enjoy projects where I can own the pipeline end-to-end: modeling the system, implementing the algorithms, and validating behavior through simulation and testing.
