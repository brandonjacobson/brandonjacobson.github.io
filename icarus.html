Overview

Icarus is a high-power rocket payload designed for controlled descent using an airbrake mechanism. I led the payload design, optimizing internal space, structural integrity, and accessibility for electronic components during assembly and flight testing.

Current Progress

Working on closed-loop control algorithms to simulate and test the airbrake’s dynamic response under varying altitude conditions using MATLAB and Simulink. Next phase includes flight data analysis and PID tuning for improved altitude precision.
